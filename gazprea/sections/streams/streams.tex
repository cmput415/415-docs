\textit{Gazprea} has two streams: stdout and stdin. They are not automatically available, cannot be
used immediately, and therefore \textit{must be} assigned to a \code{var} as so:
\begin{lstlisting}
  var out = std_output();
  var inp = std_input();
\end{lstlisting}

\assertion{The streams constructors \code{std_output()} and \code{std_input()} \textit{will not} be
used in expressions.}{assign-streams}
